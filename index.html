The Fido Manifesto
SWFTM.
Stop Working For The Man
Tens of millions of people want to work for themselves.
Only a fraction succeed.
Of the 33 million small businesses in the United States, too many fail. And even more people never get started, held back by the complexity of forming an entity, finding capital, acquiring customers, and recruiting talent. Not for lack of ambition. For lack of support.
Artificial intelligence holds tremendous promise. It also prompts a real fear: that small businesses could be rendered irrelevant, replaced by AI agents that perform better, faster, and more cheaply than human labor.
We reject that future.
Fido Financial believes that anyone with the desire and work ethic to build something of their own deserves the opportunity to succeed. That AI should be deployed not to replace human talent, but to amplify it. That technology, wielded rightly, leads to prosperity and abundance for the people who take the risk to build.
By deploying a specialized set of AI capabilities tailored to the exact pain points small business owners face, Fido becomes a limitless workforce, handling every tedious function the owner hates, so they can focus entirely on the work only they can do.
With Fido, aspiring business owners can SWFTM, knowing they have instant access to the world's top business minds to start, fund, and grow their business. A loyal companion. A relentless operator. A partner that never sleeps.
